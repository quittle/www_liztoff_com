If there is one thing that I’ve learned in my copywriting career, it is that creativity is a muscle, not a specific skill set, and an adept copywriter can flex their creativity to seamlessly meet the needs of a brand, client, or project.

I have had the pleasure of writing about the everyday brands of American flash-sale retailer Zulily, the luxury brands of the iconic London department store Harrods and everything in between. Then, in search of a new challenge outside of the world of fashion and e-commerce, I became the sole creative writer for the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA).

No matter my prior experience with the subject matter, I have found a way to create engaging experiences for audiences with a wide variety of interests and backgrounds.

Current Work for AILA

My role is to make our members aware of our benefits, events, and resources via daily marketing emails, announcements, and more.
