  • Script for This 75th Anniversary Video Message from AILA’s Executive Director


    Click to View Transcript

    Hello and welcome to AILA’s 75th anniversary celebration! I’m Ben Johnson, Executive Director of AILA, and it is my great honor and pleasure to kick off this momentous year with all of you.

    In the past 75 years, AILA has seen tremendous change both within our membership and in the field of immigration law itself; our diverse community growing from just 20 New York attorneys in 1946 to over 16,000 members around the world today. Much has changed over the years, but what has always remained constant is our members’ unyielding dedication to making a difference in the lives of countless individuals and to improving the practice of immigration law.

    AILA’s 75th anniversary provides the opportunity to celebrate our community and our shared history.

    I invite you all to explore our timeline of AILA milestones, read the reflections of past AILA presidents, to share your own AILA memories, and to learn about other ways to join in the celebration.

    Keep an eye out for events in the coming months celebrating the trials and triumphs that we have faced over the last 75 years, and for updates about the much-anticipated AILA Annual Conference in June 2022. We hope that you will join us in AILA’s birthplace, New York City, for next year’s conference, as it is bound to be another event for the AILA history books.

    Thank you for being part of this amazing community and we hope you will join us in celebrating throughout this year.
